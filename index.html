What is Web Design?

Web design is the process of planning, creating, and maintaining websites. It involves the layout and arrangement of elements on the page, choosing colors, fonts, graphics, and content to achieve a functional and aesthetically pleasing website. Web design encompasses the visual aspect of the site, as well as the user experience (UX), meaning the site should be easy to navigate and provide users with what they're looking for in an intuitive way.
